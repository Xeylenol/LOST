Rules & Regulations

Please read all rules carefully before participating in LOST

Event Details

Date

9th February 2024

Timing

10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Team Size

3-4 Members

Venue

IISER Kolkata

Registration Rules

  • Registration deadline: February 5th, 2024
  • Team size must be between 3-4 members
  • All team members must be present during registration confirmation
  • Teams must report to the LOST stall by 9:45 AM on event day
  • On-spot registrations may be available if quota is not filled
  • Valid ID proof required for all team members

General Rules

  • The event will run for approximately 6 hours
  • Results will be announced at 6:00 PM
  • Teams must stay together throughout the hunt
  • Use of personal vehicles is not allowed
  • Mobile phones are permitted but must be used ethically
  • All clues and puzzles must be solved without external help
  • Respect campus property and environment

Code of Conduct

  • Maintain decorum and discipline throughout the event
  • Follow instructions from event organizers
  • No interference with other teams' progress
  • No sharing of clues or solutions with other teams
  • Respect all safety guidelines and protocols

Grounds for Disqualification

  • Breaking any of the above rules
  • Damaging campus property
  • Cheating or unfair practices
  • Misbehavior with organizers or other participants
  • Not following safety protocols
