A Strange Signal. A Deserted City. A Deadly Secret.

In the distant future, humanity has conquered the stars. Massive structures draw on the energy of entire stars. Terraformed planets stand as new colonies for the ever-expanding reach of mankind.


A strange signal was intercepted from a colonial planet thought to be long lost. You are a group of explorers sent to determine the nature of the signal, and if the colony exists, to bring it back into the Galactic Web. However, the last thing you remember is the systems of your shuttle going haywire just as you began to enter the atmosphere.


Once you wake up, you find yourselves unable to remember anything, stranded on an unfamiliar planet, with only hours of oxygen left. In the distance you spot a city.Perhaps the city offers a way to escape this desolate world?
Welcome to LOST.
